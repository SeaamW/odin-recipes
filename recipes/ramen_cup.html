What you need:

  • A bank account with at least $1.50 in it
  • a fork
  • a method of boiling water

How to make:

  1. Remove plastic wrapping from cup
  2. Boil water
  3. Open ramen cup paper lid halfway
  4. Pour boiling water 3/4ths of cup volume
  5. place fork on top of paper lid, closing it
  6. Sit and think about your day
  7. Get whatever toppings you like (boiled egg, sriracha, beans - don't knock it till you try it, etc)
  8. Make sure you have water close by for the amount of sodium you're about to consume

Enjoy your cup noodles!
