The classic, reliable peanut butter sandwich

No jelly...don't @ me

What you will need:

  • The cheapest bread you can find, preferably whole wheat
  • Jif peanut butter, crunchy or creamy, your choice
  • The ability to eat peanuts
if you can't eat peanuts, sorry, try almond or cashew
these will be more expensive though
  1. Take sliced bread and place in front of you
  2. Take out your spoon or knife
  3. Dip into peanut butter
  4. Lick peanut butter off spoon or knife
  5. Wash the spoon or knife unless you live alone and are a little reckless
  6. Dip back into peanut butter
  7. Spread the peanut butter on the bread until covered
  8. Cover both pieces of bread with peanut butter and join them together
The most important step:
  • Cut the sandwich horizontally (yes, I did just say that)

    • Now, serve with whole milk and proceed to enjoy a classic staple!
